The Threats We Fight

📞 The Grandparent Scam

AI voice cloning makes it sound like your grandchild is in trouble. Scammers demand immediate wire transfers for "bail" or "medical emergencies."

💻 Phantom Hacker Scam

A three-phase fraud: fake tech support claims your computer is hacked, "helps" you move money to "safe" accounts, then drains your savings.

❤️ Romance Scams

Online relationships built over months lead to requests for money — often claiming a sudden emergency or investment opportunity.

📈 Investment Fraud

Promises of guaranteed returns through crypto, AI trading, or exclusive deals. Victims lose retirement savings to sophisticated schemes.
